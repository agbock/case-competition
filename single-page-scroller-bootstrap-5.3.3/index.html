About Suzanne

Suzanne Collins is a bestselling author best known for The Hunger Games trilogy, which has captivated millions worldwide and been adapted into a blockbuster film series. With a background in television writing, Collins has also authored other critically acclaimed fantasy series. Her thought-provoking storytelling blends adventure with social and political themes, making her one of the most influential authors of modern young adult literature.
